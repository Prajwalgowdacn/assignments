Chocolate Donuts

Ingredients:

  • 3 1⁄4 cups (14 oz.) bread flour, sifted
  • 3⁄4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 3⁄4 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1⁄2 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 3 oz. bittersweet chocolate, grated
  • 4 tbsp. unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3⁄4 cup buttermilk

Instructions:

  1. You can use a stand mixer or manually mix it.
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk flour, powders, soda, salt, and cinnamon in a bowl; set aside. Combine sugar, butter, bittersweet chocolate, vanilla, buttermilk, eggs, and yolks in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment; beat until smooth. With the mixer is running, slowly add dry ingredients and mix until a soft, sticky dough forms.
  3. Heat 2″ oil in a small saucepan until a deep-fry thermometer reads 350°.
  4. Using lightly oiled hands, roll about ¼ cup batter into a loose, sticky ball; pat gently into a disk. You can use an improvise cutter like a cup and a bottle lid for the center hole. Make a 1½” hole in the center of dough; carefully slide into oil and fry, flipping once, until puffed and golden, about 3 minutes. Using a slotted spoon, transfer to a baking sheet with a wire rack; repeat with remaining dough and let donuts cool completely.
